Conrad Anker

Mountaineer, Author, Philanthropist

Notable Ascents and Expeditions

  • 8 May 1987 Southeast Face Gurney Peak, Kichatna Mountains, Alaska Range, Alaska, USA. First Ascent.
  • 1 May 1999 Mallory and Irvine Research Expedition Mount Everest, Himalayas, Nepal
  • 2002 Chang Tank Trek Chang Tang Plateau, Tibet, China
  • June 2007 Altitude Everest Tibet Side Expedition Mount Everest, Himalayas, Tibet, China
  • October 2011 Meru Central Peak Himalayas, India

Writings

  • 1988 "Gumbies on Gurney".American Alpine Journal.
  • 1990 "Hunter's Northwest Face" American Alpine Journal
  • 1998 "With You in Spirit" American Alpine Journal
  • 2001 "The Lost Explorer: Finding Mallory on Mt. Everest"

"We do not just measure greatness by the peaks that we climb, or the heights that we reach, but rather by the positive impact that we create for ourselves, our communities, and our planet. Our goodness to each other is what makes us whole, and what endures for generations beyond our physical feats & achievements."

-- Conrad Anker
